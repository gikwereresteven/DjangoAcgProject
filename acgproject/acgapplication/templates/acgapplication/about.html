Project mission

The project mission is also to secure the shortage of organic fertilizers, conserving natural resources, save the environment and creation of many employments for poverty reduction and sustainable development.

Project vision

To be leader company with many industries to supply different types of fertilizers by reducing the imported fertilizers for achieving food market security and sustainable development worldwide.

Project goals

To supply the sufficient fertilizers all over the Rwandan territory by saving the environment at 70% of the needs within only 5 years and within 20 years for being the best supplier company of different types of fertilizers worldwide.
